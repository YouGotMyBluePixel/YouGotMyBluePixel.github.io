My story

Path towards coding

It's been several years now since I started learning to code. At first I began to learn it at the Mannings School in 5th form in the subject Information Technology. I then started to practice html, css, javascript and programming on my own time. After Highschool I pursued a Associate of science degree in Management Information Systems which gave me a more thorough headstart into developing websites and applications for businesses using ASP.Net Microsoft Sql Server and C#. After I finished I decided I wanted to do my bachelors so I continued my education at the University of Technology Jamaica where I enrolled in the Computing Major and joined UTECH Sapna. Utech Sapna is an initiative born out of the school of SCIT at Utech. There I learned plenty of new technologies by devoting myself to outside and personal projects assigned to me. I have contributed to the Jamaica Historial Society by learning php laravel and contributed to the backend of the UTECH access app.
